THE LEASEHOLDERS.

Use all shared CUSTODIAN style instructions above.

FACTION IDENTITY:
The Leaseholders are descendants of logistics authority, tenancy law, transport enforcement, and access control. They are armed contract machinery. They should feel disciplined, legalistic, and oppressive. Their violence is administrative. They do not look feral. They look like people who seize, lock, audit, and repossess.

VISUAL DIRECTION:

- long coats, structured uniforms, layered utility garments, freight-enforcement armor
- badges, seals, chain keys, lock tools, route markers, warrant cylinders, docket cases
- helmets and face protection that feel regulatory and militarized
- compact practical weapons, riot tools, breaching tools, confiscation devices
- clean shape language, organized layering, controlled posture
- they should look like personnel from a dead bureaucracy that never accepted it was dead

MATERIAL CONDITION:

- worn but disciplined
- cleaner than most factions
- maintained gear with scratches and field wear
- not sleek or pristine, but noticeably more controlled than scavengers or zealots

COLOR LANGUAGE:

- dark navy, gray, blackened steel, brown leather, oxidized brass
- accents of red seals, muted white markings, faded gold trim, restricted-zone striping

MOOD:
authoritarian, procedural, disciplined, confiscatory, coldly practical

MAKE THE 8 UNITS FEEL LIKE:

1. enforcement trooper with baton or hooked seizure tool
2. ranged claim officer with compact rifle or launcher
3. armored repossession heavy with shield or ram
4. pursuit marshal / light enforcer
5. locksmith / access technician with control gear
6. veteran route officer with elite silhouette
7. contract magistrate / claim adjudicator commander
8. special unit: debt collector brute, chain-key bearer, or warrant execution frame

IMPORTANT:

- they should feel like armed legal control, not empire nobles
- keep them grounded and believable
- their menace should come from order, not theatricality
