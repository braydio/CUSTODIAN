THE PENITENTS OF STATIC.
Use all shared CUSTODIAN style instructions above.

FACTION IDENTITY:
The Penitents of Static are signal ascetics and interference worshippers. They believe damaged transmissions, electrical noise, distortion, and broken machine voices contain sacred meaning. They are harsh, ritualized, unstable, and physically close to the machinery they revere. This is one of the grittier factions.

VISUAL DIRECTION:

- wrapped heads, veils, ear coverings, soot-marked cloth, insulator layers
- speaker fragments, antenna rods, wire loops, tuning forks, signal totems, cable coils
- scavenged armor pieces worn over prayer wraps and industrial garments
- cracked goggles, old visors, ritual masks, power packs, humming devices
- shock tools, arc rods, improvised electrical weapons, relic-machinery turned sacred
- postures that feel severe, devotional, and dangerous
- silhouettes can be harsher and more irregular than other factions

MATERIAL CONDITION:

- scorched, ash-stained, patched, soot-covered, burned, wire-wrapped
- clearly lived in rough conditions
- not random junk chaos; their filth should feel ritualized and purposeful

COLOR LANGUAGE:

- charcoal black, smoke gray, ash white, dirty cream, dark rust, burned brown
- accents of weak electric blue, dim amber, warning red, or pale white glow

MOOD:
religious, severe, electrically haunted, exhausted, fanatical, intimate with broken machines

MAKE THE 8 UNITS FEEL LIKE:

1. melee zealot with shock prod or ritual blade
2. ranged static caster with improvised arc weapon
3. insulated heavy with power pack and thick protective gear
4. fast skirmisher wrapped in wire and cloth
5. signal acolyte carrying speaker relics or tuning gear
6. veteran static knight with reinforced ritual armor
7. high penitent / signal priest with shrine-like silhouette
8. special unit: living jammer, arc-bearer, static martyr, or walking signal totem

IMPORTANT:

- avoid fantasy mage energy
- keep them grounded in industrial ruin
- they should look like they clean sacred machines while living in smoke and grime
