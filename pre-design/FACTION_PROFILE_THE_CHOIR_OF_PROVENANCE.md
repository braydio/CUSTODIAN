THE CHOIR OF PROVENANCE.

Use all shared CUSTODIAN style instructions above.

FACTION IDENTITY:
The Choir of Provenance is a severe archival verification force: austere, doctrinal, quiet, old, and unnervingly preserved. They are not flamboyant cultists and not glossy robots. They should feel like ancient procedure given form: restraint, quarantine, validation, seal enforcement, and purity of record.

VISUAL DIRECTION:

- structured robes, tabards, sealed coats, rigid armor frames, controlled layered garments
- restrained symmetry
- serial bands, seal devices, purity markers, archive locks, scanning apertures, restraint tools
- narrow mask-faces, faceless visors, calm unreadable optics
- clean lines compared to most factions, but still old and weathered
- integrated devices should feel archival and procedural, not flashy

MATERIAL CONDITION:

- old but maintained
- austere rather than dirty
- weathered institutional surfaces, careful upkeep, controlled wear
- less scavenged than most other factions

COLOR LANGUAGE:

- bone white, dark iron, charcoal, faded institutional gray, dull bronze
- restrained accents of pale cyan, cold green, or seal red

MOOD:
judgmental, doctrinal, calm, severe, quiet, archival, incorruptible

MAKE THE 8 UNITS FEEL LIKE:

1. melee seal enforcer with restraint staff or purity blade
2. ranged verifier with precise archive weapon
3. quarantine heavy with barrier frame
4. swift retrieval unit with narrow severe silhouette
5. provenance technician / scanner operator
6. elite canonical guard with disciplined armor logic
7. adjudicator / choir overseer with monastic authority
8. special unit: archive warden, seal-bearer construct, or doctrinal execution frame

IMPORTANT:

- this faction should be one of the least dirty
- make them feel ancient, preserved, and institutionally terrifying
- avoid sterile clean sci-fi; preserve age and weight
