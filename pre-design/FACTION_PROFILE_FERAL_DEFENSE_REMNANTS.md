FACTION IDENTITY:
Feral Defense Remnants are broken security systems, abandoned defense frames, damaged enforcers, and old institutional violence still operating under corrupted logic. They are remnants of formal authority decayed into automatic hostility. They should feel dangerous, damaged, relentless, and haunted by obsolete duty.

VISUAL DIRECTION:

- security armor, riot gear, damaged exoskeletons, compliance frames, exposed servos
- old enforcement markings, warning paint, cracked shields, impact damage, replacement panels
- integrated industrial weapons, breach tools, suppressive frames, pursuit hardware
- official silhouettes degraded by time and repeated field repair
- some units may be more machine-like, others more armored-humanoid, but the whole sheet must feel coherent

MATERIAL CONDITION:

- chipped, scarred, over-repaired, dented, operational despite damage
- not filthy cloth-grime like cultists
- more mechanical wear, impact damage, and authority decay

COLOR LANGUAGE:

- gunmetal, dark security green, faded white, black, hazard orange, dark gray
- accents of red warning lights, amber hazard lamps, faint blue optics

MOOD:
abandoned, mechanical, damaged, relentless, old-authority, hostile

MAKE THE 8 UNITS FEEL LIKE:

1. melee breaker / compliance ram
2. ranged sentry frame
3. heavy suppression unit
4. fast pursuit frame
5. repair / maintenance support unit
6. veteran compliance enforcer
7. command sentinel / old station-captain silhouette
8. special unit: cracked exosuit brute, chained gun platform, or unstable defense relic

IMPORTANT:

- not sleek robots
- make them feel like dead institutional hardware still enforcing rules no one remembers
- strong silhouette clarity for gameplay use
