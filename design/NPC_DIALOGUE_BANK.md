# Ash Bell Contiuity themed dialoge, spoken by Drifter, or similar

```

    “In elder years, the Ash Bells tolled only at the ending of an age.”

    “Now they answer one another without cease.”

    “Each cry births another ruin.”

The drifter looks toward the distant haze.

    “And still the Custodians wander on…
    stitching shut a world already given to cinders.”
```
