# Idea Backlog

Capture one sentence per idea here before deciding whether it deserves a card.

## Seeds

- Material Intelligence System: surfaces automatically drive FX, audio, decals, and destruction behavior.
- Sector Activity Simulator: distant sectors continue evolving through cheap offline simulation.
- Resource Economy Graph: production, consumption, shortages, repairs, and demand become inspectable systemic pressure.
- Faction Knowledge System: each faction tracks what it knows about the player and the world.
- Line-of-Communication Graph: power, communications, logistics, and travel routes become explicit world infrastructure.
- World Event Timeline: major global events can cascade across sectors.
- Procedural Ruin Generator: destruction is generated from simulated failure causes rather than random damage dressing.
- AI Morale and Cohesion System: groups react to losses, suppression, isolation, and leadership.
- Developer Replay System: playtests can be scrubbed through a timeline with world state and telemetry.
- Performance Budget Manager: live visualization and enforcement of CPU/GPU budgets.
