## One-line pitch

Players navigate by memorable landmarks rather than minimaps.

## Problem it solves

Large worlds become visually repetitive.

## Why it fits CUSTODIAN

The world should be readable through architecture.

## Player-facing effect

Every sector contains one dominant landmark, three supporting landmarks, and numerous local landmarks. Players naturally orient themselves.

## Systems touched

World design, environment art, level design.

## Dependencies

Biome documentation.

## Risks

Poor landmark placement weakens navigation.

## Minimal version

Apply hierarchy to Sundered Keep.

## Full version

Entire world follows landmark rules.

## Graduation criteria

Graduate during biome polish.
