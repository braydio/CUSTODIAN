## One-line pitch

Every region intentionally leaves important questions unanswered.

## Problem it solves

Overexplaining destroys wonder.

## Why it fits CUSTODIAN

The world should feel ancient and only partially understood.

## Player-facing effect

Players constantly wonder:

Why is the ocean glowing?

Who built the Return Causeway?

Why are bells still ringing?

What is offshore?

## Systems touched

Lore, environmental storytelling, dialogue, quest design.

## Dependencies

None.

## Risks

Can become confusing if there are no eventual answers.

## Full version
