## One-line pitch

Visual motifs teach gameplay expectations without tutorials.

## Problem it solves

Players should recognize danger and opportunity instinctively.

## Why it fits CUSTODIAN

The world should communicate through architecture rather than UI.

## Player-facing effect

Examples:

Broken relay tower means expect snipers.

Blue maintenance lanterns mean temporal instability nearby.

Collapsed crane means heavy enemy patrol.

Three maintenance banners mean hidden terminal cache.
