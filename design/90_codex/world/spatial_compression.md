## One-line pitch

Make the world appear significantly larger through carefully planned sightlines.

## Problem it solves

Small worlds feel small.

## Why it fits CUSTODIAN

The Return Causeway and Sundered Keep already lend themselves to this technique.

## Player-facing effect

Players see a distant destination. Hours later they arrive. Looking back reinforces the perceived journey.

## Systems touched

Level design, vista design, streaming.

## Dependencies

Major world layout.

## Risks

Needs deliberate sightline planning.

## Minimal version

Apply to opening area.

## Full version

Every biome uses spatial compression intentionally.

## Graduation criteria

Graduate before world layout is finalized.
